# BeeMind Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-06_

BeeMind ("we", "our", "the app") helps beekeepers manage their apiaries, hives,
queens, inspections, harvests, tasks, and inventory. This document explains what
data we store and how we handle it.

## 1. Data we store

When you use BeeMind, we store the following data on our backend (Supabase):

- **Account data**: email address, authentication identifiers, language
  preference, and other basic profile data needed to sign you in.
- **Beekeeping data** you create inside the app:
  - Apiaries / yards
  - Hives
  - Queens
  - Inspections
  - Harvests
  - Tasks
  - Inventory items

This data is linked to your account and is only visible to you. Row-level
security rules in Supabase ensure other users cannot read or modify your data.

## 2. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the BeeMind service: to display your
apiaries and hives, to keep your records in sync across devices, and to let you
manage your beekeeping operation.

We **do not** sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for
profiling.

## 3. Backend provider

BeeMind uses [Supabase](https://supabase.com) for authentication and database
storage. Supabase processes data on our behalf as a sub-processor.

## 4. Permissions

The app requests only the permissions it needs to function. The current MVP
does not require camera, microphone, location, push-notification, or media
library permissions.

## 5. Data retention and deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all related data at any time by
contacting support. Once a deletion request is processed, your account and
beekeeping data are permanently removed from our database.

## 6. Children

BeeMind is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect
data from them.

## 7. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated inside the
app or via email.
