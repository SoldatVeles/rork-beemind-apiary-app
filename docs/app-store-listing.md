## Long description

BeeMind is a modern beekeeping companion built for hobbyists and small-scale
beekeepers who want to stay on top of their colonies without spreadsheets or
paper notebooks.

Manage every part of your operation:

- **Apiaries / Yards** — organize your bee yards in one place.
- **Hives** — track each hive, its status, and history.
- **Queens** — record queen origin, marking color and replacement dates.
- **Inspections** — log brood, temperament, honey stores and more.
- **Harvests** — record honey, wax, propolis and pollen yields.
- **Tasks** — keep a simple to-do list per hive or yard.
- **Inventory** — manage frames, supers, sugar, treatments and supplies.
- **Multi-language** — English, Português, Español and Deutsch.

BeeMind syncs to your private cloud account so your data is safe across
devices. No ads, no data selling — just clear tools to help your bees thrive.
